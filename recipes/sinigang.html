Sinigang na Baboy

Description

Sinigang is a Filipino soup that is traditionally made with tamarind. It is very sour and comes in many different varieties, with the main add-in being meat, seafood, or vegetables. This particular version is sinigang na baboy, which is pork sinigang.

Ingredients

  • Knorr sinigang instant-mix
  • Pork belly
  • Water
  • Vegetables (string beans, leafy greens, etc.)
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook the pork meat.
  2. Mix the knorr instant-mix into water. If you have tamarinds, you can use their juice.
  3. Boil all the ingredients together.
