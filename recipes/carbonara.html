Carbonara

Description

This recipe is for the Italian version of carbonara, which does not add cream. The recipe is very simple, requiring just the key ingredients eggs, guanciale, cheese, and pepper. Don't forget the salt, of course. The dish is said to have orginated in Rome.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 pound guanciale (cured pork cheeck), diced
  • 1 (16 ounce) package spaghetti
  • 3 eggs
  • 10 tablespoons grated Pecorino Romano cheese, divided
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil on skillet and add guanciale
  2. Cook spaghetti in pot of boiling water
  3. Whisk eggs with some Pecorino Romano and some black pepper. Pour over pasta and add guanciale, eventually adding the rest of the cheese and more pepper
