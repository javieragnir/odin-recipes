Beef Wellington

Description

Beef wellington is a well-loved British dish. It is a big chunk of beef wrapped in pastry. Beef wellington is the signature dish of acclaimed chef Gordon Ramsay

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 pounds beef tenderloin
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 2 ounces liver pate
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 17.5 ounce package frozen puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 egg yolk, beaten
  • 10.5 ounce beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons red wine

Steps

  1. Preheat oven. Place beef inside with butter.
  2. Saute onion and mushrooms in butter.
  3. Mix together pate and butter. Season with salt and pepper. SPread pate over beef and top with onion and mushrrom mixture.
  4. Roll puff pastry dough over beef.
  5. Bake in the oven.
  6. Mix juices with beef broth and red wine for sauce.
